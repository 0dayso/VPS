安装路径:
    find / -name nginx.conf
    find / -name php.ini
    find / -name my.cnf
    find / -name httpd.conf
    这种方法会找出好几个文件. 要稍微判断下哪个是.

    [root@localhost ~]# find / -name nginx.conf
    /root/lnmp1.4/conf/nginx.conf
    /root/lnmp1.4/lnmp1.3-full/conf/nginx.conf
    /usr/local/nginx/conf/nginx.conf
    [root@localhost ~]#
