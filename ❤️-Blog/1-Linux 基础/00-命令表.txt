🔸 ssh密钥

    ⦿ 传输(客户端)  scp -P 2222 -r id_rsa.pub root@23.105.192.96:~/.ssh/
    ⦿ 传输(客户端)  scp -P 2222 -r id_rsa.pub root@104.224.139.45:~/.ssh/
    ⦿ 注册(服务器)  cat ~/.ssh/id_rsa.pub >> ~/.ssh/authorized_keys
